About Me

I am a Web Design, Front-End, and Data Visualization student based in Tokyo. I focus on building clear, visually driven websites and interactive visualizations that help users understand structured information.

My work combines design thinking with front-end development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, D3.js, and React. Through personal and coursework projects, I explore how layout, interaction, and data visualization can work together to communicate ideas effectively.
